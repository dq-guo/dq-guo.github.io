PhD Candidate in Political Science
Freie Universität Berlin · Berlin, Germany
I am a PhD candidate (near completion, on the job market) in Political Science at Freie Universität Berlin, and a Predoctoral Researcher at the ERC-funded project on Digital Governance in China.
My research sits at the intersection of authoritarian politics, political psychology, and technology.

Distinctive, warm, intellectual. Deep wine/burgundy is less common in academia, which makes it memorable. Subtle and sophisticated — the pink-rose background tint adds warmth without being loud. A bold choice that still reads as scholarly. Works especially well if you want your site to stand out from the sea of blue academic portfolios.

Character

Warm, personal, earthy. Deep terracotta/rust with a warm cream background gives the site a very personal and approachable feel. The warmth is inviting, and the dark burnt-orange accent is vibrant without being garish. A more daring choice — shows personality. Less "traditional academic" but still elegant and highly readable.
